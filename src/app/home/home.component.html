Twitter is a popular social media platform that enables users to send and read short 280-character messages called "tweets." It was created in 2006 by Jack Dorsey, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams and has since become a significant part of online communication and social networking.

The platform allows users to follow other accounts to see their tweets in their timeline. It's widely used by individuals, businesses, celebrities, politicians, and more to share thoughts, news, updates, and engage in conversations. Twitter also supports various media types like images, videos, and GIFs in tweets.

Twitter has been used for various purposes, including sharing news in real-time, marketing, customer service, political discussions, networking, and personal expression. Its brevity and immediacy make it a powerful tool for communication, information dissemination, and engagement.
